DBAS Framework

Crowdsourcing
Design Simulator

DBAS stands for Define · Broadcast · Attract · Select — the four stages every crowdsourcing campaign must design. Together they determine who shows up, how many, and what kind of ideas they submit.

For each of three real companies you will make 12 decisions (3 per stage), run the simulation, and see how your choices shape submission volume, idea quality, novelty, and contributor retention. Then compare across contexts and reflect.

© Linus Dahlander. Based on research with Lars Bo Jeppesen and Henning Piezunka.
